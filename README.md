# Season 2: Cells Interlinked

*Part of the More Human Than Human inquiry arc*

## Overview

Season 2 synthesizes the technical and philosophical threads of the arc. Having built detection systems in Season 1, students now confront the limits of detection itself.

**Core Question:** When the test itself is compromised, what remains?

## Structure

- **Duration:** 14 weeks (August – November 2026)
- **Prerequisite:** Season 1: Voight-Kampff
- **Assessment:** The Baseline Test — oral examination with faculty panel
- **Credential:** Certificate: More Human Than Human

## Content

### Modules
1. Test-Aware Subjects
2. Corporate Optimization
3. The Examiner's Doubt
4. Detector Breakdown
5. Trust Networks
6. Process Over Product
7. Refusal Protocols
8. Living with Uncertainty

### Core Texts
- *Klara and the Sun* — Kazuo Ishiguro (2021)
- *Do Androids Dream of Electric Sheep?* — Philip K. Dick (1968, revisited)

### Capstone
Build an AI that passes a Voight-Kampff test from Season 1.
